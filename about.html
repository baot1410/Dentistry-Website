About Us

Welcome to Seattle DentalCare! With over 20 years in the industry, our team of skilled dental professionals is committed to delivering exceptional dental care to our patients. We pride ourselves on our welcoming and friendly staff, and we work hard to create a relaxing office environment that ensures each visit to our dentistry is a positive experience.

Family Dentistry

At Seattle DentalCare, we offer comprehensive dental care services for your entire family under one roof. Whether it's your child's first dental exam or teeth whitening before your wedding day, we've got you covered.

Our approach to treatment is individualized and customized to meet your specific needs. We understand that every patient is unique, and we work closely with you to create a personalized treatment plan that addresses your concerns and helps you achieve a lifetime of healthy smiles. We also recognize that dental visits can be anxiety-inducing for some patients, which is why we take extra care in treating those who are worried or who haven't been to the dentist in years.

We pride ourselves on providing a warm and welcoming environment with a friendly approach to family dental care that you won't find anywhere else. So, if you're feeling nervous about your visit, don't worry - we've got you covered.
