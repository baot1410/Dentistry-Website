Our In-Office Membership Plan

With options for new patients!

No need for insurance to get the best care

The only plan you'll ever need

Seattle DentalCare frequently receives concerns from patients about the absence of dental insurance. However, we believe that insurance coverage should not dictate the quality of treatment you receive. Consequently, we have created a membership program, a dental savings subscription plan exclusive to our patients. The purpose of this plan is to provide affordable access to high-quality dental treatment.

Patients enrolled in this plan pay an annual fee and receive regular checkups, cleanings, and x-rays. They also have access to discounted pricing for numerous other exclusive services at our dental clinic. Unlike traditional insurance plans, our membership program does not have an annual limit, frequency limits, or waiting periods.

We recognize the importance of affordable dental care and developed this membership program to address this need. With our program, you can receive the dental care you deserve without worrying about the constraints and restrictions of typical insurance plans.
